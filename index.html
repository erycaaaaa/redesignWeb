Nutritious dish with perilla leaves

A nutritious recipe perfect for your health.

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

5 mins | Easy | 1 person

Recipe
Nutritious Dish with Perilla Leaves
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup mixed greens (spinach, kale, perilla leaves)
  • 1 boiled egg, halved
  • 1/2 cup chickpeas (cooked or canned, drained)
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup celery, sliced
  • 1 tbsp sunflower seeds
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
Instructions:
  1. Wash and dry the mixed greens, perilla leaves, and vegetables.
  2. Boil an egg for about 7-8 minutes, then slice it in half once cooled.
  3. Layer the mixed greens on a plate and add chickpeas, tomatoes, cucumber, and celery.
  4. Place the egg halves in the center, sprinkle sunflower seeds, and drizzle olive oil and lemon juice.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and serve immediately.
Sprout Salad with Soft Boiled Eggs

Sprouts and eggs for essential vitamins.

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

5 mins | Easy | 1 person

Recipe
Sprout Salad with Soft Boiled Eggs
Ingredients:
  • 1 soft-boiled egg, halved
  • 1/2 cup mixed nuts (e.g., almonds, cashews, or walnuts)
  • 1/4 cup nangka, cut into small pieces
  • 1 cup lettuce, torn into bite-sized pieces
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
Instructions:
  1. Boil the egg for about 6 minutes for a soft yolk. Cool under cold water, peel, and halve.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine lettuce, chopped nangka, and mixed nuts.
  3. Top with egg halves, drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and serve immediately.
Celery Mushroom Salad with Apples

Packed nutrients and flavor.

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

5 mins | Easy | 1 person

Recipe
Celery Mushroom Salad with Apples
Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup boiled button mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/4 cup apple, sliced
  • 1/2 cup celery leaves, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
Instructions:
  1. Combine celery leaves, apples, and mushrooms in a bowl.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  3. Season with salt and pepper, and toss gently to combine.
  4. Serve immediately.
